Detailed ROI Calculation Example (More Realistic Scenario)

This example provides a simplified framework for estimating the potential costs and ROI for a **mid-sized enterprise with 6 SOC analysts**, leveraging BytePlus AI solutions.

1. Estimated Annual BytePlus AI Costs

ModelArk (DeepSeek R1 Inference):
  • Analyst Questions (10,000/month * 12 months = 120,000 questions):
  • Cost for Questions: (120,000 * 1,500 input tokens * $1.35/M) + (120,000 * 1,000 output tokens * $5.40/M) = $243 + $648 = **$891**
  • Monthly Reports (12 reports):
  • Cost for Reports: (12 reports * 50,000 input tokens * $1.35/M) + (12 reports * 10,000 output tokens * $5.40/M) = $0.81 + $0.648 = **$1.46**
  • Subtotal ModelArk: **$892.46 / year**
VectorDB:
  • Annual Embedding (1 GB logs/day = 2M lines/day, 500 chars/line = 365 GB/year):
  • Annual Embedding Cost (365 days * 2M lines/day * 500 chars/line * $0.00015/K Tokens): (365 * 1000 M Tokens * $0.00015/K Tokens) = **$54,750 / year**
  • Annual Storage (30 days retention = 30 GB max storage): ($0.02/GB/hour * 30 GB * 24 hours/day * 365 days/year) = **$5,256 / year**
  • Annual Indexing (12 CU): ($0.25/1CU/Hour * 12 CU * 24 hours/day * 365 days/year) = **$26,280 / year**
  • Subtotal VectorDB: **$86,286 / year**
Total Estimated Annual BytePlus AI Cost (A): **$87,178.46**

2. Estimated Annual Savings & Benefits

Savings from Reduced False Positives:
  • Current time on false positives: 60% of analyst time
  • Time saved by AI: 40% of (60% of analyst time) = 24% of total analyst time
  • Total savings for **6 analysts**: **$172,800**
Savings from Faster Incident Response (Reduced MTTR):
  • AI reduces MTTR by 30% (2.1 days saved per incident)
  • Estimated avoided costs per critical incident: $150,000
  • Total savings (**1 critical incident/year**): **$150,000**
Savings from Automating Manual Tasks:
  • 10% of total analyst time automated (1,080 hours saved for 6 analysts)
  • Annual savings: **$72,000**
Savings from Avoided Breaches (Prevention/Early Detection):
  • 5% reduction in overall breach likelihood/impact
  • Estimated annual savings: **$244,000**
Total Estimated Annual Savings & Benefits (B): **$688,800**

3. Net ROI Calculation

Net Annual Benefit (B - A): **$601,621.54** ($688,800 - $87,178.46)
Return on Investment (ROI): (Net Annual Benefit / Total BytePlus AI Cost) * 100%
**689.96%**
